About

Intersectional feminist and inclusion expert with 12 years of experience advancing initiatives that empower women, girls, and marginalized groups across MENA and beyond. Skilled in feminist M&E, capacity building, GEDI/GESI analysis, and GBV programming. Since 2018, I’ve led gender components for multi-million-dollar consortia, driving evidence-based, norm-shifting program design. Currently pursuing an MA in Gender, Sexuality, and Global Politics (SOAS, London).
