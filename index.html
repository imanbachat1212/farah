About

Intersectional Feminist, Gender, and Inclusion Expert with 11+ years of experience advancing initiatives to empower women, girls, and underrepresented communities. Skilled in leading gender, disability, and diversity inclusion in multi-million-dollar consortia at national and regional levels, I have developed award-winning proposals and multi-year strategies. With expertise in proposal writing, feminist M&E, GEDI/GESI analysis, and tailored capacity building, I have trained over 1,500 staff across sectors. My work spans humanitarian and development contexts, designing GBV response programs and fostering sustainable, locally driven solutions. Committed to evidence-based, inclusive programming that challenges harmful norms and reshapes narratives.
